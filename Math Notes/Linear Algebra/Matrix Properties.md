# [[Matrix Properties]]
- $(AB)' = B'A'$ where $A$ and $B$ are matrices
- $A'A$ is symmetric
- $!(AB = BA)$ No guarantee of commutative
- $a'b = b'a$ where $a$ and $b$ are vectors
- $A(B+C) = AB + AC$
