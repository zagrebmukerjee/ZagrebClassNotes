# [[Integration by Parts]]
<span style = "font-size:120%"><i >Zagreb Mukerjee </i></span>


Suppose I have the product rule: 

$$ \frac{d}{dx} f(x) \cdot g(x) = f'(x)g(x) + g'(x)f(x)$$
Integrating both sides can get us

$$ \frac{d}{dx} f(x) \cdot g(x) = f'(x)g(x) + g'(x)f(x)$$
